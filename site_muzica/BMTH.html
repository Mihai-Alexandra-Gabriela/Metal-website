Bring Me the Horizon

Bring Me the Horizon (prescurtat BMTH) este o formatie britanica de metalcore din Sheffield, Yorkshire. Format initial in 2004, grupul este format in prezent din vocalistul Oliver Sykes, chitaristul Lee Malia, basistul Matt Kean, bateristul Matt Nicholls si Jordan Fish la clape.

Fosti membri

• Curtis Ward
• Jona Weinhofen

Albume

• Suicide Season (2008)
• There Is a Hell, Believe Me I've Seen It. There Is a Heaven, Let's Keep It a Secret (2010)
• Sempiternal (2013)
• That's the Spirit (2015)
